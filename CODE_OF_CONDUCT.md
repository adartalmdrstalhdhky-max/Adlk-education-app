# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and professional environment
for everyone contributing to the Adlik project, regardless of background or experience.

This project exists to serve education and community benefit first.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in discussions
- Providing constructive and professional feedback
- Focusing on solving problems, not assigning blame
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive language
- Personal attacks or hostile behavior
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that harms the integrity of the project or its contributors

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Any official communication related to the project

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject
any contributions or comments that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in removal from the project.
